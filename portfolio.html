About Me

I'm a proud Chicago native currently based in Huntsville, AL, blending my love for technology with creativity and problem-solving.

I graduated cum laude with a B.S. in Computer Science (Cybersecurity) from Alabama A&M University, where I led as President of the Computer Science Club and served as Treasurer of Girls Who Code.

My experience spans across internships at NASA Marshall Space Flight Center and Lockheed Martin, where I contributed to system integration, tactical testing, and software documentation under agile workflows.

What I Bring to the Table

  • Expert in system integration & tactical analysis

  • Passionate about inclusive, accessible technology
  • Developer with hands-on experience in mobile, web & embedded systems

Whether I’m coding a mobile app or architecting a secure backend, I believe in building technology that uplifts, empowers, and scales with purpose.
